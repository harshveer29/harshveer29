## About
 
CSE student at Galgotias University (2026 batch), currently working as a **Frontend Developer at Monkify.ai**, building AI-powered recruitment workflows with Next.js, React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS.
 
Alongside that, I founded and lead technical development at **BunkToBrains**, an EdTech SaaS platform, and take on freelance full-stack work when the scope is interesting.
 
I'm a fresher looking for an SDE / full-stack role at a product company or funded startup — but I'd rather let what's shipped below speak for that than a list of adjectives.
 
## What I'm building
 
| Project | What it is |
|---|---|
| **TryLuvify** | A SaaS platform for personal websites — love pages, wedding invitations, portfolios, event pages — with AI-assisted content generation. **Live, in production.** I founded it and own the full stack. |
| **BroPinger** | A cross-platform messaging app: real-time chat, stories, short video. Currently in **closed beta with real testers** — actively iterating based on what breaks. |
 
Both are early-stage. No vanity metrics here — what I can say is they're deployed, used by real people, and still mine to fix when something breaks at 2am.
